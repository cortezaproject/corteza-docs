= Namespace permissions

== Any namespace

.To set permissions for any namespace:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. click on the *lock icon* on the top of the *namespace list*.

include::ROOT:partial$permissions/compose-namespaces.adoc[]

== Specific namespace

.To set permissions for a specific namespace:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. click on the edit icon when hovering over a namespace,
. click on the *lock icon* on the top of the *namespace list*.
