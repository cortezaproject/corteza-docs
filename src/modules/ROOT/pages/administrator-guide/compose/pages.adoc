= Page permissions

== Any page

.To set permissions for any page:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Pages" subpage,
. click on the *lock icon* on top of the *page list*.

== Specific page

.To set permissions for a specific page:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Pages" subpage,
. click on the *lock icon* next to a page.
