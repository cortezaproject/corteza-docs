= Chart permissions

== Any chart

.To set permissions for any chart:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Charts" subpage,
. click on the *lock icon* on top of the *chart list*.

== Specific chart

.To set permissions for a specific chart:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Charts" subpage,
. click on the *lock icon* next to a chart.
